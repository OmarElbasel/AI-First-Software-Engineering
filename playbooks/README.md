# Playbooks

Step-by-step processes for recurring engineering situations.

The difference from the other assets: **chapters** teach judgment,
**templates** are documents you copy, **checklists** verify a moment in
time — a **playbook** walks you through a whole situation from start to
finish, telling you which template or checklist to reach for at each step
and what "good" looks like before you move on.

Playbooks are opinionated by design. Where a chapter presents trade-offs,
a playbook picks a default and tells you when to deviate.

## Writing a new playbook

Add one when the same situation has come up three times and the steps are
stable. Each step must state why it exists and what done looks like —
a numbered list of commands without reasoning is a script, not a playbook.
